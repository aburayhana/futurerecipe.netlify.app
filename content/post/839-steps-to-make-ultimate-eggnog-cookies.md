1. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla extract
1. Prepare 2 yolks
1. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
1. Take 2 1/4 cup flour

##### Instructions to make Eggnog Cookies:

1. Cream sugar and butter until light

1. Add flour mixture &amp; beat @ low speed
1. Don&#39;t over mix
